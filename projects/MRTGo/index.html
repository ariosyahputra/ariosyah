MRTGo - Exit easily, travel safely

Overview

MRTGo is an additional feature that is expected to be integrated into the MRT-J application to make it easier to determine the fastest and most efficient route for users to reach their destination using MRT transportation. The feature also provides a list of landmarks that users can visit based on the selected MRT station and notify users prior to arrival in station to desired landmarks.

Problem Statement

To enhance the experience of searching for exit gates in MRT stations and provide greater convenience to passengers, our product aims to streamline the process. We understand that knowing the specific exit gate related to certain attractions is crucial for MRT passengers. This information not only helps them choose the most efficient route but also saves time, energy, and effort in reaching their desired destinations.
