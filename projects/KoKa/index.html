KoKa (KosaKata)

Overview

KoKa is an interactive educational platform designed to assist hearing parents in teaching vocabulary to their deaf children. The platform focuses on immersive, story-based learning experiences and engaging quizzes to enhance the child's vocabulary acquisition.

Problem Statement

Deaf children are difficult in learning languagle due to limitations in absorbing linguistic exposure from their environment and often misinterpret linguistic input. A major challenge deaf face is a limited vocabulary, especially when it comes to expressing abstract ideas because it needs context and lack of visualization. Enhancing their grasp of abstract language can contribute to the advancement of their literacy skills and self actualization.
